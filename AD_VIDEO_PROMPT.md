# MathPad — Ad Video Prompts (for an AI video generator)

Copy-paste prompts for tools like Sora, Veo, Runway, Kling, or Pika. Story:
a frustrated kid doing math on paper → mom introduces MathPad → the kid happily
practices the *same* handwritten math on the iPad.

Most tools generate short clips (~5–8s), so build the ad from the shots below
and stitch them in an editor. Tips at the bottom.

---

## Global style (prepend to every shot)

> Warm, cinematic, photorealistic home setting. Soft natural window light, cozy
> and premium, shallow depth of field, gentle film grain. Authentic and
> heartfelt (not stocky). Calm, uplifting mood. Smooth slow camera moves. 4K.
> [Aspect ratio: 9:16 vertical for social — or 16:9 for YouTube.]

---

## Full version (~20s, 4 shots)

**Shot 1 — Frustration (≈5s)**
> An 8-year-old child sits at a wooden kitchen table doing math homework on
> paper. Pencil columns, long-division scribbles, eraser shavings, a crumpled
> sheet nearby. The child sighs, furrows their brow, taps the pencil, slumps and
> rests their cheek on one hand, looking stuck and tired. Close, empathetic
> framing on the messy worksheet with crossed-out attempts, then the child's
> frustrated face. Muted, slightly cooler light.

**Shot 2 — Mom arrives (≈5s)**
> A warm, reassuring mother walks into frame, smiles, and gently sits beside the
> child. She places a hand on their shoulder, then brings out an iPad/tablet and
> turns it toward the child. The child looks up, curious. Soft, hopeful lighting
> begins to warm. Tender, close two-shot.

**Shot 3 — The app, by hand (≈6s)**
> Tight over-the-shoulder close-up of the child's index finger writing a number
> directly on the iPad screen, the handwritten digit appearing crisply on a
> clean notebook-grid math app as they trace it — exactly like writing on paper,
> but on the tablet. The child's eyes widen and they smile, fully engaged. Mom
> watches proudly beside them. Cozy golden light.

**Shot 4 — Joy & resolution (≈5s)**
> The child confidently writes answers on the iPad, smiling and focused; a small
> gentle moment of success (a soft green checkmark glow, a tiny sparkle — subtle,
> not flashy). The child looks up at mom with a proud grin; mom smiles back.
> Pull back to the warm, tidy table — the paper struggle is gone. Calm,
> satisfying final beat.

## Practical production notes

- **The app on screen:** AI video **cannot** render MathPad's real UI — it will
  invent a fake math app. Best result: let the AI generate the "finger writing
  on a tablet" close-up, then **composite a real screen recording of MathPad**
  onto the iPad in your editor. (Or shoot that shot as a genuine screen recording
  and intercut.)
- **No AI text:** generators garble on-screen text. Add **all captions, logo,
  and the end card in post.**
- **Casting/tone:** natural, warm, real — avoid stocky "thumbs-up" clichés. Aim
  for genuine "in the zone" learning.
- **Music:** soft, warm, uplifting acoustic or piano; light, hopeful.
- **Continuity:** keep the same child, table, and lighting across clips so the
  cuts feel like one story. Generate a few takes per shot and pick the best.
- **Brand palette (for captions/end card):** see `AD_BRIEF.md` — per-operation
  accents, calm premium look, the abacus logo.
