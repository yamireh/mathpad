## Description (≤ 4,000 characters)

```
MathPad — practice math by hand.

Most math apps ask kids to tap the right answer. MathPad asks them to work it
out — by hand, just like on paper.

Kids write each digit with their finger, and MathPad reads their handwriting.
They carry tens, borrow, and line up long division in a built-in notebook
scratch area — learning the real method, not just the answer.

WHY PARENTS LOVE IT
• Real pencil-and-paper math: column addition and subtraction with carrying and
  borrowing, multi-step multiplication, and full long division.
• Show your working: a notebook-style worksheet for every step.
• Watch how to solve: a friendly animated hand demonstrates each method, step by
  step.
• Gentle hints: one tap reveals just the next step — never the whole answer.
• Calm and ad-free: no ads, no flashing rewards, no pressure.
• Private and offline: works without internet and collects no personal data.

MADE FOR AGES 5–12
Adjustable difficulty grows with your child: choose the number of digits, how
many questions, an optional timer, and options like decimals, remainders, and
negative answers. A friendly results screen celebrates progress and lets kids
review and fix their work.

FREE TO START
Addition is completely free, with all of its features. Unlock Subtraction,
Multiplication, Division, and Mix together with a single one-time purchase —
no subscription, ever.

Build real math confidence, one handwritten problem at a time.
```
