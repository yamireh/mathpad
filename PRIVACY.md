# MathPad — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 3, 2026**

Thank you for using MathPad. We built MathPad to be a safe, calm place for
children to practice math by hand. Your privacy — and your child's privacy — is
important to us, and we have designed the app so that it collects **no personal
data at all**.

## The short version

**MathPad does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.**
Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

## Information we collect

**None.** MathPad does not ask for, collect, or process any personal
information. Specifically:

- We do **not** require an account, sign-in, name, email, or phone number.
- We do **not** collect location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers.
- We do **not** use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDKs that
  collect data.
- We do **not** send any information off your device. MathPad works fully
  offline and makes no network requests for the practice experience.

## Information stored on your device

To make the app work, MathPad saves some information **locally on your device
only**, where it never leaves the device and is never accessible to us:

- Your practice settings (such as number of digits and question count).
- Your handwriting and answers for the current session.
- Your past session history and scores.

This data lives in the app's private storage. You can remove it at any time by
clearing the app's history within MathPad or by deleting the app, which removes
all of its local data.

## Children's privacy

MathPad is intended for children, including children under 13. Because the app
collects **no personal information whatsoever**, it does not knowingly collect
data from anyone, including children. There are no ads, no in-app messaging, no
social features, no external links presented to children during practice, and no
data sharing with third parties. This is consistent with the U.S. Children's
Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU General Data Protection
Regulation provisions for children (GDPR-K).

## Third parties

MathPad does not share data with third parties because it does not collect any
data. The app does not integrate advertising networks, analytics providers, or
social media services.

## Purchases

Addition practice is free. The other operations are available as paid in-app
purchases — you can unlock the full bundle of the remaining operations, or buy
each operation bundle on its own. All purchases are handled securely by the App
Store; MathPad does not receive, collect, or store any payment information.

## Changes to this policy

If we ever change how MathPad handles information, we will update this page and
revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected here
before they take effect.
